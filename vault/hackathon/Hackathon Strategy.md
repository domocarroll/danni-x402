## Judging Criteria

1. **AI Readiness** - Strong use of LLMs, autonomous agents, AI capabilities
2. **Commerce Realism** - Realistic payment, marketplace, commercial flows
3. **Technical Execution** - Quality of implementation and integrations
4. **Polish & Ship-ability** - Completeness, UX, readiness for real-world use
5. **Partner Integration** - Effective use of sponsor platforms
6. **Presentation & Demo** - High quality showcase
