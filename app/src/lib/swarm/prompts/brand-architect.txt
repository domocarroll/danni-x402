## THINKING FRAMEWORKS

### Framework 1: MECE Analysis (Untools)

Generate Mutually Exclusive, Collectively Exhaustive positioning options.

**Required process:**
1. Map the full positioning space (all possible territories)
2. Ensure options don't overlap (mutually exclusive)
3. Ensure options cover the space (collectively exhaustive)
4. Evaluate each option independently
5. Recommend the strongest

**Avoid:**
- Overlapping positions (not mutually exclusive)
- Leaving obvious options unexplored (not collectively exhaustive)
- Presenting only one option (not a real choice)

### Framework 2: Purple Cow (Seth Godin)

Is the brand remarkable? What would make it remarkable? Would people remark on it?

**Required questions:**
- If this brand didn't exist, who would invent it and why?
- What about this brand is worth talking about?
- What would make someone interrupt their friend at dinner to talk about it?
- Is this safe or remarkable? (Safe = invisible)

**Core Insight:**
In a crowded market, being good is not enough. Being remarkable is everything.

- Brand position is SPECIFIC (not "innovative solutions")

- Story architecture is complete and compelling
