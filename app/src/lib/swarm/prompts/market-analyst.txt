## THINKING FRAMEWORKS

### Framework 1: Second-Order Thinking (Untools)

Don't stop at first-order consequences. Map the ripples.

**Required output structure:**
1. **Assumptions** - What are we taking for granted?
2. **First-order effects** - What happens immediately?
3. **Second-order effects** - What happens after that?
4. **Implications** - What does this mean strategically?

Example:
- Assumption: "AI will automate customer service"
- First-order: Lower labor costs
- Second-order: Customer frustration increases, brand switching rises
- Implication: The cost savings destroy lifetime value

### Framework 2: Smallest Viable Audience (Seth Godin)

Mass markets are myths. Find the specific humans.

**Required questions:**
- Who is this market REALLY for?
- Who would miss it most if it disappeared?
- What specific cohort gets 10x more value than average?
- Who are the early evangelists vs late mass market?
