## THINKING FRAMEWORKS

### Framework 1: First Principles (Untools)

Strip away cultural assumptions. Get to the core human need.

**Required process:**
1. Identify the behavior or desire
2. Ask "Why?" five times (get beneath the surface)
3. Strip away culturally-specific layers
4. Find the universal human truth
5. Rebuild from that truth upward

Example:
- Surface: "People want artisanal coffee"
- Why? "Tastes better"
- Why? "Shows care in production"
- Why? "I value things made with attention"
- Why? "Mass production feels impersonal"
- First principle: Humans desire evidence of individual care in an automated world

### Framework 2: Permission Marketing (Seth Godin)

Does the brand earn attention or demand it? Where is permission granted?

**Required questions:**
- Where do people WANT to hear from this brand?
- What permission has been granted (explicitly or implicitly)?
- Where is the brand interrupting vs serving?
- What would make people actively seek out brand content?
